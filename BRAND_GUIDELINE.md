# DIOS HABLA – Brand & Style Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence

**Purpose:** Provide a hub for learning about the Bible, the gospel, and connecting with local churches or missionaries.

**Tone:** Inspirational, modern, hopeful, and clear.

**Core Values:** Light, truth, connection, authenticity, accessibility.

**Emotional Goal:** Inspire curiosity, evoke peace and purpose, invite reflection and action.

---

## 2. Color System

### Primary Palette

- **Celestial Blue** (`#1E88E5`): Truth, clarity, trust
- **Sunrise Gold** (`#FBC02D`): Hope, joy, divine presence
- **Pure White** (`#FFFFFF`): Light, purity, openness

### Secondary Palette

- **Earth Clay** (`#8D6E63`): Cultural rootedness, humanity
- **Emerald Green** (`#43A047`): Growth, renewal
- **Deep Indigo** (`#283593`): Wisdom, depth

**Color Ratio:** 60% neutrals, 30% blue/gold, 10% accents

---

## 3. Typography

- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold (modern, geometric)
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro or Open Sans
- **Highlights/Quotes:** Playfair Display for emphasis

### Type Scale

| Element | Size |
|---------|------|
| H1 | 48–60px |
| H2 | 32–40px |
| Body | 18–20px |
| Captions | 14px |

---

## 4. Imagery Style

### A. Visual Identity

All images must communicate **light, hope, and accessibility**. Visual tone is modern, inclusive, and peaceful.

**Primary use cases:**
- **Illustrations:** for teaching and conceptual topics (e.g., salvation, grace)
- **Photography:** for real-life ministry and community content

### B. Photography Guidelines

**Use when:** portraying people or community moments

**Style:**
- Natural light (prefer golden hour)
- Warm color grading, high realism
- Unposed, authentic emotions
- Backgrounds simple and bright
- Represent Latin American diversity

**AI or sourcing keywords:**
> "Natural light photography, golden hour, Latin American people, documentary style, warm tones, shallow depth of field, hopeful mood."

### C. Illustration Guidelines

**Use when:** depicting biblical or spiritual concepts

**Style:**
- Modern flat-vector or soft-gradient realism
- Simplified forms, minimal outlines
- Gentle texture, balanced composition
- Blue, gold, and white palette emphasized
- Faces optional or abstract for universality
- Avoid cartoon exaggeration or high saturation

**AI reference keywords:**
> "Modern Christian illustration, flat vector, soft gradients, minimalist design, warm light, blue and gold palette, geometric composition, spiritual symbolism, no text."

### D. AI Image Generation Standards

**Always include:**
- Brand colors (blue, gold, white)
- "No text" and "consistent lighting"
- Symbolic realism (hands, light, open path, Bible, sunrise)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 for thumbnails
- Center-weighted composition

**Example AI prompt:**
> "Modern minimalist Christian illustration of divine light and hope, open Bible with light rays, blue and gold palette, flat vector style, soft gradients, 16:9 ratio, no text."

---

## 5. Iconography

**Style Guidelines:**
- Simple line icons with rounded corners
- Use metaphors of light, growth, guidance (sun, seed, bridge)
- Maintain consistent 2D flat design
- Primary colors: blue or white

---

## 6. Tone & Messaging

**Voice:** Warm, clear, invitational

**Style:** Positive, concise, action-oriented

**Examples:**
- "Luz para tu camino."
- "Explora el mensaje que transforma vidas."
- "Encuentra una comunidad cerca de ti."

---

## 7. Logo System

**Primary Mark:** Dios Habla wordmark in Montserrat Bold, with accent color in Sunrise Gold

**Symbol:** Open Bible emitting sound waves and light rays (representing hearing and revelation)

**Usage:**
- Symbol alone for icons
- Full wordmark for headers and print

**Backgrounds:** white, light gray, or dark blue only

---

## 8. Thumbnail & Graphic Layout

**Specifications:**
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9
- **Focus:** Centered subject or symbol
- **Background:** Subtle gradient (blue–gold–white)
- **Overlays:** Light geometric shapes or rays
- **Avoid:** Heavy textures, cluttered scenes
