##  Brand Essence

Purpose: Inspire exploration of the Bible and connection to local faith communities.
Tone: Inspirational, modern, hopeful, and respectful across diverse cultural contexts.
Core Values: Light, truth, connection, authenticity, accessibility.
Emotional Goal: Spark curiosity, evoke peace and purpose, invite reflection and action.

## Color System
Primary Palette

Celestial Blue (#1E88E5): Represents divine truth, clarity, trust.

Sunrise Gold (#FBC02D): Hope, new beginnings, joy.

Pure White (#FFFFFF): Light, purity, simplicity.

Secondary Palette

Earth Clay (#8D6E63): Connection to land and people; cultural rootedness.

Emerald Green (#43A047): Growth, life, creation.

Deep Indigo (#283593): Depth, wisdom, mystery.

Usage Ratios: 60% neutral (white/earth), 30% primary blue/gold, 10% accent (green/indigo).

## Typography

Headings: Montserrat – clean, modern, geometric, easy to read in Spanish.

Body: Source Sans Pro or Open Sans – balanced for web legibility.

Scriptural or Highlight Text: Playfair Display – elegant serif for gravitas.

Hierarchy Example:
H1: 48–60px, bold Montserrat
H2: 32–40px, semi-bold Montserrat
Body: 18–20px, regular Source Sans Pro
Captions: 14px, italic or light

## Imagery Style
General Direction

Themes: Light emerging from shadow, people in natural connection, joy, reflection, Scripture in life context.

Composition: Center-weighted subjects, soft natural lighting, wide depth-of-field for warmth and realism.

Color Grade: Warm tones; golden-hour light; gentle contrast. Avoid oversaturation.

Photography Examples

Smiling families and individuals in natural light.

Landscapes (mountains, coastlines, cities) symbolizing God’s creation.

Close-ups of hands, Bibles, community action.

Modern, modest dress styles that reflect diversity.

Illustration or Graphic Overlays

Minimal geometric accents (lines, circles, light rays).

Abstract gradients in brand colors for thumbnails.

Avoid heavy textures or retro filters.

## Iconography

Line-based icons with rounded corners.

Use metaphors of light, growth, connection, and guidance (sun, path, seed, bridge).

Maintain 2D flat consistency, using primary blue or white for visibility.

## Tone & Messaging

Voice: Warm, clear, invitational—not preachy.
Style:

Positive and action-oriented (“Descubre,” “Conéctate,” “Explora la Biblia”).

Short, uplifting headlines.

Blend spiritual curiosity with personal relevance.
Examples:

“Luz para tu camino.”

“Explora el mensaje que transforma vidas.”

“Encuentra una comunidad cerca de ti.”

## Thumbnail / Graphic Consistency

Base Template: 16:9 ratio, centered subject or text overlay.

Background: Use subtle gradients (blue–gold–white).

Text Overlay: White or dark blue with semi-transparent backdrop for readability.

Consistent Visual Motifs: Rays of light, open books, hands, paths.
